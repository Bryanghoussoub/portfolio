The Administration Experience

While bankers are operating their daily work, management are looking for development. Our Administration Experience provide the management with a detailed analysis and smart reporting, helping them to track, build and innovate. In Addition, our solution will help them predict future actions by using our new Data Insights tools.

Overview

Explore you branches by managing existing products, reviewing the existing workflow along with the upcoming tasks and monitoring their latest activities. Customizable Dashboard will help you personalize it with unlimited analysis services.

Smart Reporting

Inspect your business performance using our Smart Reporting System, by visualizing live and in-depth insights and metrics through multiple type of charts.

User Management

One Place to manage all your users, groups and identities. A Secure container to store username and passwords and integrate group policies for each position and service.

Product Builder

Following all insights generated and personal bankers’ reviews, available data will suggest products to be build which may satisfy the bank needs. Management will be able then to create new products and launch them through our Product Builder.

Users & Permisions

Detailed access and permission levels will help structuring the solution in order to reduce all risks by fine-tuning user’s ability. Management of accounts, groups and users will also reflect an organized structure.

Product Sales

Product Sales will provide details products report in terms of sales and engagement. This will lead to more understand customers need for future improvements. In Addition, targeting reports can be generate monthly and yearly.

Track activities

Latest activities can also be monitored by management staff. For administration purpose, it can be also filtered between customers view and employees view.
